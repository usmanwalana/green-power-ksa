    About Us

    Green Power Trading

    Green Power Trading (EST) as a company is one of the well-known importers and distributors of heavy equipment spare parts in Saudi Arabia. Combined with our global partner companies, we serve the entire country with our widely connected network.

    We provide a wide range of automotive spare parts and quality customer service to the wholesale, retail and government markets. Our major range of products includes Bobcat, Case, Perkins, JCB, Kubota, DEUTZ, Oil and Hydraulic seals, Filers, Belts, Anti-Vibration Mountings, and Engine Parts. Our central bases and exclusive distributorship right in Riyadh and Jeddah offers us an obvious advantage to provide quality parts at the right price and time. We import the best parts and accessories from trusted sources in USA, Italy, Japan, Thailand, China the U.K, Germany. Our teams of experts have over 20 years of experience in this market. Our professional management team and trained, customer-service oriented sales personnel are highly qualified to meet your needs. Our central warehouse located in Riyadh having an inventory of more than 10,000 products is working 12-hours a day to meet with the customer orders and giving the logistic support. Located in the central region Green Power enjoys the advantage of fast logistic service around the kingdom. We welcome all startups to build and strengthen their ties with Green Power Trading.

    Products

    We deal in following products

    BOBCAT

    Bobcat needs no introduction for Saudi Arabian market. Skid loaders and tele handlers are famous and widely acknowledged. Green Power Trading is amongst the only spare parts importers for bobcat skids and tele handlers.

    JCB

    JCB has made a great debut in construction sector over the last decade. The machines includes backhoes, telescopic forklifts, wheel loaders and some newly designed skids. Green Power offers genuine and replacement parts for all models and makes.

    DEUTZ

    Deutz engines are widely acknowledged in the kingdom. The models range from one cylinder two huge twelve cylinders engines. Green Power Trading provides the genuine Deutz parts along with excellent quality replacement parts.

    CASE

    Case construction and agriculture machinery in Saudi Arabia is one of the very first machinery introduced in the kingdom. Its vast range of models and tough transmission is well appreciated all over the kingdom. Green Power has the edge of over 20 years of experience in Case machinery. Green Power Trading supply the original parts with shortest lead time.

    Kubota

    Kubota engines are widely distributed in all machinery sectors. Their finely designed engines have the quality of less fuel consumption. Green Power Trading offers a variety of genuine and replacement parts for all Kubota engines.

    Perkins

    Perkins spare parts are also available at Green Power Trading.
